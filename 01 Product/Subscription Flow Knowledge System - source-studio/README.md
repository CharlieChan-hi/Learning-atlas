## What This Is For

- Designing AIGC photo/video app flows.
- Deciding whether an app should use result-first onboarding or questionnaire onboarding.
- Understanding why users pay for subscriptions.
- Building loading, onboarding, paywall, and payment flows as one complete decision system.

## Main Principle

Users do not pay because a paywall is beautiful. They pay when the product has already made a believable promise, reduced enough uncertainty, and made the next step feel worth it.

> 中文说明：用户不是因为订阅页好看才买单，而是因为前面的价值展示、信任建立、风险降低和价格解释连在一起成立了。
